Betting Tips

Basic terms in soccer betting

H(1)-> Home team

X -> Draw

A(2) -> Away team

1X -> This means the home team either wins or draws.

X2 -> This means the away team either wins or draws.

12 -> This means either team wins.

BTS -> Both teams to score.

OVER 1.5 -> This means at least two goals will be scored, apply the same logic to others.

UNDER 1.5 -> This means less than two goals will be scored, apply same logic to others.

HANDICAP-> This means you are giving a team goals ahead of the match, so lets assume
you have something like handicap 1:0 it means you have given the home team one goal ahead
of the match.
